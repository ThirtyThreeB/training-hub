When to retest
Time-based: Every 4–6 weeks during base/strength phases. During peak (wks 9–11) avoid testing — preserve fatigue for race sharpness. Taper week: test only if no race within 10 days.
Performance signals: Retest when your avg watts on Zone 2 rides feel easy at a heart rate well below threshold, or when you're regularly hitting Z5 power in what should be Z4 intervals.
